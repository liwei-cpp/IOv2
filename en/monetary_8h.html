Detailed Description

Defines the monetary<CharT> class, the user-facing facet for monetary formatting and parsing. It snapshots all locale data from monetary_conf<CharT> at construction and provides put and get overloads for outputting and parsing monetary character sequences, supporting both national and international format modes.
